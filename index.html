What do we

do?

ALIEN INNOVATIONS is an Organization focused on tackling real-life challenges encountered by visually impaired individuals. Our commitment lies in enhancing visually impaired community life by harnessing innovative technologies. We strive to foster independence and create solutions that prioritize comfort and self-reliance.

VISION

Our vision is to empower

visually impaired individuals

Our vision is to empower visually impaired individuals, integrating them fully into society and opening up new opportunities. We are dedicated to enhancing their safety and confidence, enabling self-reliance, and fostering a sense of belonging. Through innovation and compassion, we strive to create a world where everyone, regardless of their visual abilities, can lead independent and fulfilling lives.

MISSION

We are on the mission to make the lives of the visually impaired people self-dependent. We want to make them lead a normal life with our advanced assistive products. 
              

                
                    WORLD’S FIRST COMPACT DEVICE
                
              

              
EMPOWERING VISUALLY IMPAIRED

              
 ENHANCING MOBILITY









NAVINAUT is a torch type assistive device device for the visually impaired people to make them walk obstacles free without the use of the stick. NAVINAUT helps them walk obstacles free by detecting the obstacles in advance.

NAVINAUT is a torch type assistive device device for the visually impaired people to make them walk obstacles free without the use of the stick. NAVINAUT helps them walk obstacles free by detecting the obstacles in advance. While walking the device detects the obstacles in advance and vibrates to alert them. The design of the device gives them the feasibility to carry it easily and use it in multi directions to detect the obstacles and walk. The device makes them not hit any obstacles and walk safely. NAVINAUT, with its impressive 45-day battery backup, promotes user adaptability, offering a straightforward and reliable solution for enhanced navigation and mobility for the visually impaired.
